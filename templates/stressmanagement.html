Stress Management

Here are some suggestions tailored to your recent spending:

  • Create a Budget: Track income and expenses to understand where your money is going and avoid overspending.
  • Set Financial Goals: Prioritize needs (tuition, rent) over wants and set aside money for savings.
  • Use Student Discounts: Take advantage of discounts on food, transport, and entertainment to save money.
  • Limit Non-Essential Expenses: Cut down on unnecessary spending like frequent eating out or impulsive shopping.
  • Build an Emergency Fund: Save a small amount regularly for unexpected expenses.
  • Avoid Credit Card Debt: Use credit cards wisely, paying off the full balance monthly to avoid high-interest charges.
  • Plan for Big Expenses: Save in advance for textbooks, laptops, or trips instead of relying on loans.
  • Seek Financial Advice: Attend workshops or consult advisors available through your institution.
